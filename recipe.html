Fluffy Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Milk
  • 1 tbsp Butter or Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black Pepper (optional)
  • Chopped Onions, Tomatoes, and Coriander (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium flame.
  3. Pour the egg mixture and let it cook undisturbed for a minute.
  4. Gently lift the edges and fold the omelette once the bottom sets.
  5. Serve hot with toast or enjoy plain.
